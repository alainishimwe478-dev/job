The Mountain Tea Difference

Grown in the rich, volcanic soil of Rwanda's high-altitude mountains, our tea leaves develop a complex and robust flavor profile unlike any other. We partner with local farmers to ensure fair trade practices and sustainable farming, bringing you a cup of tea that is as ethical as it is delicious.

Connecting Farmers to the World

High-Quality Production

We work with farmers and factories across Rwanda, like Gisovu and Kitabi, to ensure our teas meet the highest standards of quality, flavor, and aroma.

Responsible Tea Exportation

Our commitment to ethical practices extends beyond our farms and into the global market through responsible tea exportation. By managing a direct-trade process, we ensure fair prices and transparency for our farmers, empowering their communities and supporting their livelihoods. Our export operations are built on a foundation of quality control and efficiency, allowing us to deliver the finest Rwandan tea to customers worldwide while upholding the environmental and social standards that define our brand. This not only builds a reputation for superior tea but also for a business that truly makes a positive impact, from the hills of Rwanda to your cup.

Sustainable Partnership

Our business is built on a foundation of ethical practices and community empowerment. We invest in local communities and uphold environmentally-friendly farming methods.

Global Exportation

We manage the entire process, from farm to factory to your cup, offering seamless exportation of premium black and green orthodox teas to the global market.
